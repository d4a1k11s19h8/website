About Us

Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology (VNIT) is a premier technical institute located in Nagpur, Maharashtra, India. Established in 1960 as a Regional Engineering College, VNIT is now one of the National Institutes of Technology (NITs), under the Ministry of Education, Government of India. It offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various fields of engineering, architecture, and management. The campus is spread over 215 acres, providing a conducive environment for learning and research. VNIT is known for its strong emphasis on innovation, research, and quality education. It has state-of-the-art laboratories, libraries, and sports facilities, catering to the overall development of students. The institute boasts a highly qualified faculty with expertise in diverse domains. VNIT regularly collaborates with industries and research organizations for academic and technological advancements. The campus is home to various student-driven clubs, technical fests, and cultural events that encourage creativity and teamwork. VNIT's alumni network is vast and influential, with graduates holding key positions in top global companies and institutions. The institute’s vision is to produce competent engineers who can contribute significantly to society through their technical expertise and leadership qualities.
